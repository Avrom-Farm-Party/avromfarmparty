About the Farm

Avrom Farm is the former home of renowned sculptor-painter Lester Schwartz. Today, Lester’s grandson Hayden Holbert runs it as a holistic, regenerative agricultural practice. Since 2008, Hayden has grown Avrom Farm into a full-time operation, supplying farmers’ markets and restaurants in Milwaukee, Appleton, and Chicago with exceptionally flavorful and nutritionally dense food.
