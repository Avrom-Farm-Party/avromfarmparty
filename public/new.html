Friend of the Fest

A little more about the fest…

In the 1950s, artist Lester Schwartz purchased a dairy farm in Green Lake, Wisconsin.

Lester built towering sculptures on the farm as well as paintings that are collected in the Smithsonian.

Today it’s a regenerative agricultural practice run by Lester’s grandson, Hayden Holbert.

And Avrom Farm Party is our celebration of everything the farm stands for: nutritious, sustainable food, art, and community. ✌️
