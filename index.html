Avrom Farm Party

A family-friendly music and food festival in the heart of Wisconsin.

August 8, 2020

In 19XX, renowned artist Lester Schwartz established Avrom Farm in Ripon, Wisconsin.

The farm was his workshop for towering sculptures and … paintings.

Today, it’s a regenerative, pasture-based farm run by Lester’s grandson, Hayden Holbert.

Avrom Farm Party is our celebration of regenerative farming, great music, and community. A day and night of … .
