Avrom Farm Party

Avrom Farm Party 2020 is canceled.

We’re sad to say that due to COVID-19, we’re not able to put on this year’s festival.

But we’re already looking forward to the next time it’s safe and feasible for us to get together.

If you purchased a ticket, we’ve already initiated your refund. You should see it show up on your statement by July 20. If you don’t see the funds by then, contact us.

If you like, you can spend your would-be ticket amount on the Avrom Farm online store, which is the best way to help keep the farm afloat during this time.

Thank you for supporting the festival these past two years. We really can’t wait till we can enjoy all this food, music, and company together again.

Thank you,
Hayden and Spencer
